Our Commitment To Privacy
This Cookie Policy explains how Senda Ventures Ltd uses cookies and similar technologies on our website https://senda.ventures in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us understand how the site is used, remember your preferences, and improve your experience.

Types of Cookies We Use
We use the following types of cookies:
Strictly Necessary Cookies: These are essential for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems Performance Cookies: These allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site Functionality Cookies: These enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization Targeting/Advertising Cookies: These may be set through our site by our advertising partners and help deliver relevant ads.

Legal Basis for Using Cookies
We use strictly necessary cookies on the basis of our legitimate interest. For all other cookies, we rely on your consent, which you can provide when you first visit our website via our cookie banner.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences
You can control and manage cookies in the following ways: Via our cookie banner when you first visit our site At any time by clicking [“Cookie Settings”] on our website Through your browser settings – most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies.
