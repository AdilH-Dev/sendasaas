Introducing SENDA

Senda is a white label Chrome extension that accountants can brand. Senda allows your clients to create a shortcut to their bank*. When they need to pay an overseas supplier, they pull down the extension, make their payment (at great rates) and get back to their core business. It's fast and easy-to-use allowing your clients to make payments with minimal disruption to their workflow.
